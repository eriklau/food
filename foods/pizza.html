kiwi pizzea

HOME
  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices of kiwi
  • 15 slices of worms
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated

pizza sauce

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

how to maek

  1. Preheat the oven to 500F for about 5-10 min
  2. Make the sauce, stir together all the pizza suace ingredients
  3. roll the dough with flour until its nice and doughy and then flatten out like a pizza, preferably 14 cm in diameter
  4. spread the sauce nice and even on the dough and then place your sliced worms on
  5. cook the pizza preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes until crust is golden brown
  6. take the pizza out and place your sliced kiwis and let it cool
  7. enjoy your awesum pizza!
