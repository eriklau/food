INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb ground beef 80/20.
  • 2 tbsp butter.
  • 1/2 yellow or white onion sliced.
  • 8 oz mushrooms sliced.
  • 4 hamburger buns with sesame seeds.
  • 4 tbsp ketchup.
  • 4 tbsp mustard.
  • 4 leaves iceberg lettuce.

how to maek

  1. put ground beef on gril 5 min per side until temp reach 160F
  2. melt butter on skillet over medium heat and add onion slices and mushrooms and cook for 5 min
  3. assemble borger: bottom bun with 1 tbsp of ketchup and mustard, 1/5 mushrom onion mix, burogr, 1 lettice, 1 tomoto, 4 picle, end with 1 tbsp mayo on top bun
  4. enjoy!!!!!! :)
